Frequently asked questions

Is Nimbus a bank?

Nimbus is a financial technology company. Banking services provided by partner banks; deposits are FDIC‑insured up to $250,000.

Are there any hidden fees?

No hidden fees. We show fees upfront and most transfers are free.

Does Nimbus support business accounts?

Yes. We offer business accounts with multi‑user controls and invoicing.

How do I switch from my current bank?

Open an account in minutes and use our guided switch tool to move direct deposits and recurring payments.
